The Basic Strategy

Stand

You have a ten and a two. The dealer is showing a six. You should stand.

Why?
Hit

Double

Card Counting

A count is simply a way of tracking what cards have passed by in play and what cards are yet to come. This number can then be used to inform decisions on betting.
